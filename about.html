About the Festival

Origin Festival is a 3‑day celebration of music, art and conscious living. We bring together international and local artists across multiple stages, immersive art installations and workshops focused on sustainability and wellness.

Location

Nestled in the scenic Eden Valley, the festival campsite is surrounded by mountains and rivers—perfect for a summer escape.

Why Origin?

We believe in unity, freedom of expression and starting the new year with positive energy. Expect mind‑blowing visuals, world‑class sound, and a community you’ll never forget.
